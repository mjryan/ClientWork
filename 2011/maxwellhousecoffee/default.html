    Meet the Houses

    Adamsville Recreation Center GA Renovations will improve access, usability and help them expand services to more area residents.
    The Advisory Committee of Liberty City Youth FL Updating their facility will help them keep pace with the community as needs shift and change.
    Austin Town Hall Park IL Help this house enrich the lives of residents through the power of the arts.
    Brightwood Community Center IN Renovations would bring the center up to date and create a space as bright and invigorating as their staff.
    C. E. McGruder Family Resource Center TN Renovations will have an immediate effect on the health of the community.
    Community Education Center PA Help them continue to be a place to nurture and support aspiring artists.
    The Grow Haus CO Renovations will help develop a community storefront market in an area that has no access to basic resources.
    Mill Brook Community Center NY Support would help turn the dream of a better life into the reality of a college education.
    Sabathani Community House MN Renovations will help homeless residents practice new skills that could lead to gainful employment.
    WATTS COMMUNITY CENTER (WLCAC) CA Help this house turn resident talents into marketable skills.

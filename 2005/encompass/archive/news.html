Please join us in helping youth displaced by Hurricane Katrina. We welcome donations of school supplies, new socks and underwear, new shirts and belts in all sizes.

You can also make a financial contribution and let us do the shopping. Please go to our donation page - every cent of your donation will be used to purchase needed items. When you get to the donations page, select "Teen Clothes Katrina Relief" from the drop-down menu.

If you would like to contribute goods or volunteer to assist with this project, please call us at 626-358-3442

The Compassion Plays productions have been designed and staged to work well in a wide variety of spaces, from small classrooms to fully-equipped theatres. With advance notification, we will try our best to adapt to whatever space resources you can provide. At minimum, the "stage" area should be no less than approximately 16 X 16 feet. We will need easy access to this area in order to set up the scenery and sound equipment. Stage lighting is not necessary. The room should be well-lit and there must be an electric outlet available for our use near the staging area. Seating should be arranged to allow audience participants an unobstructed view of the performance. (Of course, we're delighted to perform in fully-equipped theatre spaces with full stage lighting when they are available!)

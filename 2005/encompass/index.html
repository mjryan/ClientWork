SPECIAL PREVIEWS OF WHEELS NOW AVAILABLE

WHEELS is a funny and thought-provoking play that gives audience participants new insight into the complex issue of immigration. Shows that occur by December 16, 2005 will be free of charge. That is a savings of $400 - $600 per session. All we require is that you provide a staging area of 16 X 16 feet, an electric outlet, 30 –100 audience members and a time block of 55 – 90 minutes. We will provide the play, the set and a professionally trained facilitator who will lead the discussions. For more information, call Jenna Delgado at 626-358-3442.

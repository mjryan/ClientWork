The Compassion Plays program is designed for 8th â€“ 12th grade audiences as well as adult audiences.

In order to maximize the impact of the Compassion Plays performances and especially the pre and post-show discussions, ENCOMPASS limits audience size to 100 people or less. We have found that larger numbers greatly diminish the opportunities for participation in the facilitated conversation before and after the play. On rare occasions, we will allow a performance for larger audiences. Please be aware that we will most likely need to charge you for a second show if your numbers exceed 100, and that a larger block of time may be needed.

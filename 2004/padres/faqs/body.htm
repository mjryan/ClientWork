Who is Canum Entertainment?
Canum Entertainment is a leading publisher of legacy sports, entertainment and children’s books.

Can I buy the book offline (in a retail store)?
The Sweet Spot is available at the PETCO Park retail stores as well as in bookstores everywhere.

What credit cards do you accept?
We accept VISA, Mastercard, American Express and Discover.

Is this a secure site? Is it safe to order online?
Yes. Ordering through our site is absolutely safe. Our fulfillment company uses 128-bit encryption to ensure that your personal information is kept private for purposes of this order only. An ICSA certified firewall appliance is used to guarantee security. This firewall blocks traffic to unused ports in order to prevent unauthorized access. All traffic is logged and we continually update our servers with the latest service security updates. Additionally, credit card numbers are not stored. A transaction code received from the credit card company is retained for order identification.
